Asian Sesame Ginger Dressing

Categories

Top Tags

Tags: Salad Dressings

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp agave nectar
  • 3 tbsp tamari
  • 2-inch piece of fresh ginger, peeled and finely grated (or roughly chopped if using blender)
  • Grated zest and juice of 1 lime

Directions

Whisk ingredients or combine in blender.
