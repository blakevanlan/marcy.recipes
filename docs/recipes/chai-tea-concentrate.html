Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 1 cinnamon stick, broken
  • 1 t. cardamom seeds
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 2-3 pieces candied ginger
  • small piece nutmeg, broken up
  • 1 whole star anise
  • 3 bags black decaf tea

Directions

Bring all ingredients except tea to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 10 minutes. Remove from heat. Add tea bags, cover and steep 5 minutes. Strain and store in a glass jar in the refrigerator. (I usually put some of the cinnamon stick and/or ginger pieces back into the concentrate to deepen the flavor over time.

To serve, fill each mug 1/3 full of chai, add milk to fill mug and sugar if desired. Microwave until warm. Grate fresh nutmeg on top to serve.
